About us

Building health literacy with accessible AI

AiHealthMate is a proof-of-concept platform that shows how machine learning and clinical knowledge can guide people toward better health awareness—without replacing medical experts.

AI

AI + Clinician insights + Safer care decisions

What AiHealthMate stands for

Practical education

We translate medical jargon into friendly language so anyone can learn from their symptoms and understand when to seek professional care.

Responsible AI

Every assessment combines model predictions with red-flag rules to keep safety at the center of the experience.

Data mindfulness

Only essential, anonymized details are collected; users stay in control of their history and can clear it anytime.

Who we’re building for

AiHealthMate lets anyone explore how AI can support everyday well-being decisions—while reminding users to rely on licensed clinicians for actual care.

Learners

Discover how symptoms relate to conditions and learn the basics of triage.

Families

Get guidance on when to monitor at home and when to book an appointment.

Developers
