AiHealth Privacy Policy

Last updated: Oct 27, 2025

Overview

This Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Data We Collect

  • Account: Google profile ID, name, and email after you sign in.
  • Usage: device/browser info and interactions for analytics and security.
  • Health Inputs: symptoms you enter and results generated by the app.

How We Use Data

  • Provide and improve the Service and personalize your dashboard.
  • Maintain security, prevent abuse, and troubleshoot issues.
  • Communicate about updates (you can opt out of non-essential emails).

Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share with service providers who process data on our behalf (e.g., hosting, analytics) under appropriate safeguards, or when required by law.

Retention

We keep data while you maintain an account or as needed for the purposes above. You may request deletion of your account data.

Security

We use technical and organizational measures to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Your Rights

Subject to your region, you may have rights to access, correct, export, or delete your data. Contact us to exercise these rights.

Children

AiHealth is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes

We may update this Policy. We will post the updated version here with a new “Last updated” date.

Contact
